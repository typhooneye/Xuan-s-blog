About

自我介绍/博客简介

欢迎来到我的博客！我叫纪璇，璇与北斗七星的第二颗星“天璇”重名，纪与北斗七星的第三颗星“天玑”同音，我很乐意别人叫我璇玑~我目前是天文学本科学生，我着迷于天文学研究的各个领域。这个博客记录了我的一些科普文章。在这里，有趣的知识应有尽有！从星座到恒星结构与演化，从天体测量对人类的影响到各种天文仪器设备，还有天文圈的大新闻和小笑话……希望你们在这里能领略到些许天文的魅力！

About

More About Me and the Blog

Hello, there! My name is Xuan Ji. In Chinese, Xuan is also the name of βUMa which is the second star in the Big Dipper. I am an undergraduate majoring astronomy. All fields of astronomy attracts me. This blog contains some of my popular science articles. Here, you could find the interesting knowledge from constellation to stellar structure and evolution, from astrometry to instrument, as well as some big news and jokes! I'll try my best to share the charm of astronomy with you guys :P.

文章

我的目标是让所有人(不管有没有天文背景)都能读懂并且喜欢这些介绍天文知识的文章，让所有人都能领略天文之美。如果你发现任何错误，或者有任何问题，或者只是对文章不满意，请一定要告诉我！希望大家不吝评论~此外还有我撰写的一些较为浅显的调研报告，不过用语比较正式，可能会有些无聊:P
My goal is to let every one understand and enjoy my articles, regardless of their background. If you have some questions or find some mistakes or just feel discontented, please do not hesitate to let me know! I appericiate your comments! Beisides, there are also some easy-to-understand reviews or reports, while they are formal style, which might be boring :P.
